Stock Market Analysis Dashboard

Stock Price Visualization and Predictions using Excel

Introduction

This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard that tracks and analyzes stock market data, including Opening Price, Closing Price, High, Low, and Volume over time. Using statistical techniques like Regression, Markov Chains, and Moving Averages, the dashboard helps visualize trends, volatility, and potential market movements from raw stock data.

Objective

To analyze and track stock price movements, trends, and volatility using Excel’s statistical and visualization tools, providing insights to better understand market behavior and support data-driven investment decisions.

Methodology

- Collected and cleaned raw stock market data from Kaggle using Excel.
- Calculated key metrics such as Opening, Closing, High, Low prices, Volume, and Daily % Change.
- Applied statistical techniques like Regression Analysis, Markov Chains, and Moving Averages to study trends and predict future movements.
- Created interactive charts, slicers, and dashboards for dynamic visualization of stock performance.
- Used conditional formatting to highlight volatility and significant price changes.

Results

The final dashboard offered clear insights into stock price trends, daily volatility, and market patterns. Users could quickly identify upward or downward trends, assess risk, and make informed investment or trading decisions.

Summary

This project demonstrates how Excel can be used to analyze and visualize stock market data. The interactive dashboard turns raw price and volume data into actionable insights for understanding trends, volatility, and market behavior.
